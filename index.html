Pizza Crust

The crust is made from dough. The dough is the whole foundation for the pizza. Pizza dough is a soft, airy base made up of flour, water, and salt along with yeast to help the dough rise. It has the ability to be stretched out and baked under a certain temperature for a certain amount of time. After it is baked, what is called a Maillard reaction occurs and browns the crust.
